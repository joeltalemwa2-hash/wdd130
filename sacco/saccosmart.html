// System Modules

Everything a SACCO
needs to operate

A complete ecosystem of tools built specifically for Ugandan cooperatives and SACCOs.

👥
Member Registry

Complete member profiles with ID verification, share history, and contribution timelines. Supports bulk import and member portal access.

members.registry
💰
Savings & Deposits

Real-time deposit tracking, automated SMS receipts, and scheduled contribution reminders. Supports multiple savings product types.

savings.core
🏦
Loan Management

Loan application, approval workflows, disbursement, repayment scheduling, and arrears management with configurable interest models.

loans.engine
📊
Financial Reports

Automated balance sheets, income statements, trial balances, and member dividend statements. Export to PDF or Excel in one click.

reports.finance
📱
Mobile Money Integration

Accept deposits and process repayments directly via MTN MoMo and Airtel Money. Real-time reconciliation included.

payments.mobile
🔔
Notifications & Alerts

Automated SMS and in-app alerts for due dates, deposits confirmed, loan approvals, and overdue repayments.

notifications.sms

// Reporting Engine

Reports that
regulators trust

📑
One-Click Regulatory Reports

Generate MoFPED and BOU-compliant reports at the end of every period. No manual data entry or spreadsheet manipulation needed.

📈
Dividend Calculations

Automatically compute member dividends based on shareholding and deposit history. Print individualized dividend statements in bulk.

🔍
Audit Trail

Every transaction is logged with timestamps, user IDs, and before/after values. Full audit trail available for internal and external audits.

📤
Excel & PDF Export

Export any report to Excel for further analysis or PDF for printing and sharing. Branded with your SACCO logo.
