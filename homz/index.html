Article 3 & 4 — Vision & Mission

Hope is built family by family.

Our vision is a community where every family has access to education, health information, and economic opportunity — not as separate programs, but as one continuous chain of support.

Our mission is to empower vulnerable families and children through education support, HIV/AIDS awareness, and community development programs that put resources directly into the hands of the people who need them.

Five objectives, one constitution.

Article 5 of our constitution sets out exactly what we exist to do. Every project we run traces back to one of these five lines.

5.1 / 5.3

Identify & Support Families

We find vulnerable families and children with school fees and scholastic materials, and conduct ongoing community outreach and aid.

5.2

HIV/AIDS Awareness

We promote prevention, counselling, and direct support to individuals and families affected by HIV/AIDS.

5.4 / 5.5

Self‑Reliance & Partnership

We initiate small income‑generating projects and partner with government bodies, NGOs, and well‑wishers to mobilize resources.

Article 6 — Membership

Open to anyone willing to support the work.

Membership is open to any individual aged 18 or above who is willing to support the objectives of the Organization. Members are asked to conduct themselves with integrity and respect the values of Homez, in keeping with our constitution.

Whether you want to volunteer locally, partner as an NGO, or support a specific family's school fees, there's a place for you here.
