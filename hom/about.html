Who We Are

Our name, where we work, and the vision and mission that guide every program we run in Masaka City, Uganda.

Article 1 & 2 — Name & Location

Homez Community Development Initiative

The organization is formally known as Homez Community Development Initiative, referred to throughout our constitution simply as "the Organization." That name carries a deliberate idea: development starts at home, in the household and the immediate community, before it can scale outward.

We are headquartered in Masaka City, Uganda, with operations extending to surrounding sub‑counties as approved by the executive committee — keeping us close to the families we serve while leaving room to grow where the need is greatest.

A Message From Our Leadership

Built by the community, for the community

Every objective in our constitution was written with one purpose: to make sure families in Masaka City have a real, lasting way forward — not a one‑time handout. Whether it's a school‑fees payment, a counselling session, or a small business loan, the goal is always self‑reliance.

We're proud of what's been built so far, and we're just getting started. Thank you for being part of it.
