Who We Are

A community‑driven organization creating lasting change

Homez Community Development Initiative is headquartered in Masaka City, Uganda, with operations extending to surrounding sub‑counties. We work alongside vulnerable families and children to strengthen access to education, health information, and economic opportunity.

Our vision is simple: a community where every family has what it needs to move forward — not handed down, but built together.

What We Do

Our Core Programmes

Integrated approaches addressing the needs of vulnerable families in Masaka City and beyond.

Education Support

School fees and scholastic materials for vulnerable children, keeping them enrolled and learning.

HIV/AIDS Awareness

Prevention, counselling, and direct support to individuals and families affected by HIV/AIDS.

Community Outreach

Ongoing household visits and aid to identify and support vulnerable families directly.

Economic Empowerment

Small income‑generating projects that promote self‑reliance and reduce household poverty.

Strategic Partnerships

Working with government bodies, NGOs, and well‑wishers to mobilize resources for the community.

Local Reach

Headquartered in Masaka City with operations extending to surrounding sub‑counties.
