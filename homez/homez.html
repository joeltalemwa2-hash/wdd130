Site Purpose

The Homez Community Development Initiative website will serve as an information and outreach platform for the organization. It will provide details about the vision, mission, objectives, membership, leadership structure, community projects, donations, and volunteer opportunities.

Target Audience

  • Community members
  • Volunteers
  • Donors and sponsors
  • Government agencies
  • NGOs and development partners
  • Families seeking support services
  • Students and beneficiaries

Vision

A community where every family has access to education, health information, and economic opportunity.

Mission

To empower vulnerable families and children through education support, HIV/AIDS awareness, and community development programs.

Objectives

  • Support vulnerable children with school fees and scholastic materials.
  • Promote HIV/AIDS awareness and counseling.
  • Conduct community outreach and aid programs.
  • Support income-generating activities.
  • Partner with NGOs, government bodies and well-wishers.

Leadership

  • Chairperson
  • Vice Chairperson
  • Secretary
  • Treasurer
  • Mobilizer

Membership

  • Open to individuals aged 18 years and above.
  • Members uphold integrity and organization values.
  • Membership may be terminated for misconduct.
