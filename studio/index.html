Our Story

Born to Build,
Built to Last

TALJOE Studios was founded with a singular conviction: that world-class software can be built anywhere — including East Africa. Founded by Joel Talemwa, the studio started as a bold experiment in a small workspace and is still growing into a full-fledged team of engineers, designers, and strategists.

We believe technology is not just a tool but a force for transformation. Every application we ship carries the ambition of our team and the trust of our clients. We work hard, iterate fast, and never compromise on quality.

Governance Model

How We Operate

Executive Committee

The CEO, CTO, and COO form the executive committee, setting strategic direction and approving major decisions. Monthly reviews keep the studio aligned with its mission.

Technical Council

Lead engineers and the CTO collaborate to set technical standards, review code quality, and make architectural decisions that guide all projects.

Project Guilds

Each active project has a dedicated guild — a cross-functional team with a project lead, developers, and designer — operating with autonomy and accountability.
