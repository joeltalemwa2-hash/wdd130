Our Mission

Adventure & Respect, Hand in Hand

Our mission is to provide unforgettable rafting adventures while respecting nature, promoting teamwork, and delivering fun and safe experiences for everyone who steps into one of our rafts.

We believe that the river teaches us as much as it thrills us — every trip leaves our guests with stories, skills, and a deeper appreciation for Uganda's spectacular waterways.

Whether you're six or sixty, first-timer or seasoned paddler, you belong on the water with us.

Est. 1998

A Quarter Century on the Nile

Founded in 1998 by a small group of river enthusiasts who fell in love with the power of the White Nile, White Water Rafting Co. has grown from weekend excursions into a fully-fledged adventure company trusted by thrill-seekers from around the world.

Over 26 years, we've guided more than 10,000 adventurers through Uganda's legendary rapids, built a team of world-class safety officers, and earned a reputation as the most trusted rafting outfitter in East Africa.
